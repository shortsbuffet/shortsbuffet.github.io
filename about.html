Shorts Buffet is a start-up fashion business in the Philippines that sells quality shorts through social media platforms such as Facebook. We mass produce an assortment of shorts for everyday use. From swim wear to sleep wear, our shorts are made of comfortable fabric that anyone will love. Our designs cover several fashion statements - whether you're a basics kind of gal or a pattern loving guy, we got you.

Comfort, aesthetic, and quality. These shorts are crafted from quality fabrics. Rock your OOTD with our pieces without emptying your pockets.

Shorts Buffet started in 2016. The owner, Emmerie De Guzman, came up with an idea to make use of her mother's excess fabrics. Since shorts are one of the most timeless fashion pieces, she decided to go with distinct yet versatile styles to be able to use all of the extra materials. The products became a huge hit and was continuously in demand. Thus, she started the business called Shorts Buffet.
